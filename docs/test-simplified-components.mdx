## Main Content

When you want to build a robot, you need to think about how it will understand the world around it. Robots use special sensors to see and feel things. These sensors are like your eyes and hands.

A robot needs a brain to think about what it sees. The robot brain is called a processor. It takes information from the sensors and decides what to do next.
